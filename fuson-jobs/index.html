复星集团2018英国校招启动，漂洋过海，10月伦敦面试，现场直通OFFER！

作者：至今 2017-09-16
  • 复星集团
  • 招聘会


关于复星集团

作为福布斯全球企业500强、中国企业前50强的复星集团创建于1992年，并于2007年在香港联交所主板上市，目前总资产超过5000亿元人民币（约合750亿美元）。复星集团致力于成为全球领先的专注于中国动力的投资集团，先后投资复星医药、复地、豫园商城、建龙集团、南钢联、招金矿业、海南矿业、永安保险、分众传媒、Club Med、Folli Follie、复星保德信人寿等。

Fosun International Limited is a family focused multinational company that has been listed on the main board of the Hong Kong Stock Exchange (00656:HK) since 2007. Founded in 1992, Fosun’s total assets exceed RMB500 billion (c.US$75 billion). With its roots in China, and through technology and innovation, Fosun’s mission is to create customer-to-maker (C2M) ecosystems in health, happiness and wealth, providing high-quality products and services for families around the world.

2018全球校园招聘计划

绝无仅有的英国留学生专场三大特权！这一次，复星集团将携2018年度最佳管培生职位——Fo-Star Program，和Fo-Star Plus Program，远渡重洋，来赴英国。

Fo-Star Program

We invest heavily in supporting and training our “Fo-Stars”. The new hires will benefit from the training and development modules in the first year, ranging from military training camp, cross-functional job rotation, financial training, executive mentorship, to more advanced programs about leadership skills as they progress further. Since 2013, the program has been held for 5 successive years, with over 100 “Fo-Stars” trained.

骨干计划Fo-Star Plus Program

Fo-Star Plus is Fosun’s premier development program for high potential individuals seeking a career in the investment industry. Successful candidates will be hired into one of Fosun’s core sectors, including healthcare, technology, tourism, and insurance, and receive executive mentorship and cross-functional job rotation. Upon successful completion of the one-year program, Fo-Star Plus graduates will be managing direct investments and cross-border M&As.

这是英国留学生独有的Priority。

特权一： 飞过来VS飞回去

绝无仅有的机会，复星集团漂洋过海，飞来英国。从开始网申到最终拿到Offer，全程在英国即可完成。无需再飞回国参加面试！一年仅此一次！

特权二： 半年VS一个月

同所有顶级管培项目一样，从投递简历到几轮面试到最终offer，整个流程需要接近半年的时间。 唯有此次的英国专场，复星集团特别开通绿色通道，9月投递简历，10月参加英国校园线下活动，现场面试，直通offer！ 一年仅此一次！

特权三： 海量专属内推机会

为英国留学生特别开放的内推投递通道，大大提升拿到面试机会，限时限量，不可错过！ 一年仅此一次！

复星集团英国校园行程

本次活动即内推通道面向全英所有校园和学生，凡网上报名通过后，即可选取适合站点参与线下活动宣讲、面试环节！

3，直通OFFER！
